Take pictures of the birds you see during your day... or night and upload them to Whistly.

With these pictures we hope to make people conscious of our feathered neighbors!

Step one - Hide & Seek

The first step is to locate the bird and this can be tricky! A Few birds are exhibitionists but most are quite shy on cameras and reluctant to show off their beauty for stranger. Be very quite and try to mingle to their environement not to disturb or send away our little friends!

Step Two - Be Patient!

The second step is to be very patient and let the bird feel comfortable with your presence. A startled bird will not pose for your lens and will probably just fly away. Take your time and it will be reward with the most beautiful pictures.

Step Three - Share the best pictures!

The third step is easy: Select the best pictures you took and share with our community! Just login and start uploading. Share as much detail you can about the bird and the place you took the picture. We are more than happy to receive your contribution!
